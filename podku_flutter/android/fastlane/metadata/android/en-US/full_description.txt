Podku – Your Podcasts, Your Server
Podku is a fast, lightweight client for your self-hosted Podku podcast aggregator. Built for people who want full control over their listening experience without sacrificing speed or simplicity.
Connect to your own Podku server and enjoy:

Instant playback with smooth, responsive controls
Clean, distraction-free interface
Background playback with full media notification support
Quick episode browsing and search across your subscriptions
Reliable syncing with your self-hosted backend

No ads. No tracking. No third-party servers — just your podcasts, streamed directly from infrastructure you own.
Podku is designed to stay out of your way: open the app, hit play, and listen. Simplicity and speed, every time.
Requires a self-hosted Podku server. Not affiliated with any commercial podcast platform.
